Our Story

15 Years of Excellence

Isaacs and Partners Pty(Ltd) was born out of necessity and passion. With over 15 years of hands-on experience running successful salons in the vibrant South African beauty market, we experienced the daily struggles of salon owners firsthand.

We tried every system on the market. Some were too complicated, some were too expensive, and none truly understood the unique dynamics of the local market. So, we built our own.

IsaacsPOS is built by salon owners, for salon owners. We understand that your business is not just about transactions; it's about relationships, creativity, and efficiency.
